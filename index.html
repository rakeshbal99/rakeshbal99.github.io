Puri and nearby Konark together house some of the most breathtaking beaches and exquisite temples in all of Odisha and are absolutely worth a visit!

I blog sometimes, usually compiling my personal experiences. However, occasionally I am compelled to give those experiences a poetic touch. Feel free to read a bit more about me!
